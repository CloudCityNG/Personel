Current Classes

 

The information of the on-going semester clclasses will be displayed, when clicked on current clclasses. These details include the course name, the class details, etc.

 

The instructor can launch the course by clicking on the link and also view the student progress.

Grade Submission

 

Grade Submission page allows the Instructor to submit grades to individual students based on their performance in examinations.

 

View Grades

 

When clicked on 'View Grade' the following page is displayed where the Instructor can -

 

1.Apply the filters to manage the view of submitted grades.

2.Search for individual student grades

3.Navigate between the pages

Submit Grades

 

Navigate to the page by clicking on 'Submit Grades' tab.

 

To submit grades -

1.Select School

2.Select Semester

3.Select Class

4.Enter marks

5.Click on submit button

Upload Grades

 

The Instructor can upload grades in bulk by clicking on 'Upload Grades' tab and uploading the file that is in the given format.
