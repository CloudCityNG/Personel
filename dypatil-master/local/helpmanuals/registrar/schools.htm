Schools

A School is defined as an institute that offers programs. CobaltLMS offers to manage multiple schools.

 

You are here: University Structure > Schools

 

The Registrar can access the Schools page by clicking on 'Schools/Colleges' as shown below or can even access it through left menu.

 

 

 

View Colleges

When you click on 'View Colleges', you will be directed to the following page. Click on symbol to delete the school, symbol to edit the school, to inactivate the school.

 

 

 

Add new

 

Click on 'Add new' tab to create a new school. The following page will be displayed.

 

 

•Select the parent school

•Enter the School name

•Enter description for the school

•Select type Campus or University

•Click on 'Save changes' to create new School

 

 

Assign Registrars

 

When clicked on 'Assign Registrars' tab, the following page will be displayed where you can assign registrars to school(s).

 

 

To assign registrar(s) to school(s) -

1.Select the registrar(s)

2.Select school

3.Click on 'Assign Registrars' button.

 

Click on symbol on the top of the page across a school to unassign registrar from a school.
