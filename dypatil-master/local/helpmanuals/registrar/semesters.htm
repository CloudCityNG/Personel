The academic year of each program is divided into semesters. Each academic year may have 2 or 3 semester.

 

You are here: University Structure > Semesters

 

To access Semesters page you may click on 'Semesters' as shown in the image below or through the left menu.

 

 

You can view the following tabs when you click on 'Semesters'.

 

View Semesters

 

When clicked on Semesters menu you will be directed to the following page where the list of all the semesters with their particulars created for a school.

 

 

Click on symbol to delete the semester or symbol to edit the semester or  symbol to inactivate the semester.

 

Create Semester

 

You will be directed to the following page when you click on 'Create Semester' page.

 

 

To create a semester -

•Select School(s)

•Enter Semester name

•Pick Start date

•Pick End date

•Enter minimum credit hours for the semester

•Enter maximum credit hours for the semester

•Enter description, if required

•Click on 'Create' button

 

 

Current Semester

 

Click on 'Current Semesters' to view the list of ongoing semesters. You can also manage (edit/delete/inactivate) these semesters.

 

View Screenshot

 

 

Upcoming Semester

 

Click on 'Upcoming Semester' tab to view the list of upcoming semesters. You also have an option to manage (edit/delete/inactivate) these semesters.

 

View Screenshot

 

 

 

Report

 

Semester report displays information on number of students enrolled/probated/completed/dismissed for each semester. To access semester report, click on 'Report' tab.
