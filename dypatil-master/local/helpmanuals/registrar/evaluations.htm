Registrar can determine the criteria to evaluate students and instructors through evaluations.

 

You are here: University Structure > Evaluations

 

Evaluations can be of types such as instructor - instructor evaluation, student - instructor evaluation, course evaluation.

 

Under Evaluations, registrar has access to the following:

 

1.Apply filters to manage the view of evaluations list that are created

 

2.Navigate between the pages

 

3.Actions: Can delete () or edit () or inactivate () an evaluation

 

4.Can view the details of 'evaluation', by clicking on the name
