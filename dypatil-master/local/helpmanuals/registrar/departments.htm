Departments

 Top  Previous  Next

Schools have various departments. The registrar can assign a department to multiple schools.

 

You are here: University Structure > Departments

 

To access the departments page, the registrar has to click on 'Departments' as shown in the image below.

 

 

View Departments

The following page will be displayed when you click on 'View departments' tab. Click on symbol to delete the department, symbol to edit the department, to inactivate the department.

 

 

•Click on download button to download the departments list.

 

 

Create Department

 

Click on 'Create Department' tab to create a new department.

 

 

To create new department -

•Select School

•Enter department name

•Enter department Id

•Enter description for the department

•Click on create department

 

 

View Instructors

 

View Instructors tab allows you to view the list of instructors assigned to different departments. Click on symbol to unassign instructor from a department.

 

 

If you wish to assign a new instructor to a department, then click on 'Assign new instructor' button. This will take you to the following page.

 

 

Assign new instructor:

 

•Select the school. This displays the list of instructors available.

•Select instructor(s)

•Select department

•Click on assign to department button.

 

Assign Schools/Colleges

 

You can also assign department(s) to school(s). The following page will be displayed when you click on Assign Schools/Colleges tab.

 

•Assigning school(s) to a department is very simple. Click on select drop down and select the school. You can view the assigned school(s) across each department.

 

 

Click on symbol to unassign schools from a department.

 

 

Upload

 

If you have more number of departments and you want to add them at a time, then this is possible.

 

Click on 'Upload' tab and you will be directed to the following page.

 

 

To upload the departments -

•Click on Choose a file button and select the file. You can even drag and drop the files.

•Select CSV delimiter

•Select Encoding

•Select preview rows

•Click on upload button

 

If your upload is successful , then you will view the uploaded departments under 'View Department' tab.
